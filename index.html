Measuring the Teen Brain in Flux

Our brain imaging research shows significant changes in brain shape, function and ability throughout the teenage years and into adulthood. These changes can serve to either promote or prevent life success. The aim of this study is to develop materials for understanding how community and family life can prime the teenage brain for successful future outcomes.
